Frequently Asked Questions

What is BookNest?
BookNest is a cozy home for book lovers! You can browse, track, and discover books and authors, join groups, and explore curated collections like Nobel Prize winners.
Can I see books that have won the Nobel Prize?
Absolutely! Our Nobel Prize section includes award-winning authors, biographies, and key literary works.
How do I get updates on new books?
Subscribe to the newsletter at the bottom of the page to stay in the loop on releases and literary insights.
Is BookNest free?
Yes! All features are 100% free — no hidden fees, no premium subscriptions.
Can I track books I’ve read?
Yes, once logged in, you can add books to your reading list and keep track of your progress.
